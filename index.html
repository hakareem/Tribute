Elon Reeve Musk is a South African-born Canadian-American business magnate,engineer and inventor.

He is the founder, CEO and CTO of SpaceX and electric car maker Tesla. Educated at the University of Pennsylvania in physics, Musk started getting his feet wet as a serial tech entrepreneur with early successes like Zip2 and X.com.He was instrumental in creating the company that became PayPal. Musk has behaved in eccentric ways from time to time and has identified himself as having Asperger's syndrome.
